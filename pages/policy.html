Our Policies

Your safety, comfort, and convenience are our priority. Please read our policies carefully.

Booking & Reservation

  • Reservation requires at least 2 hours notice.
  • Bookings can be made online or by phone.
  • Group bookings (10+ passengers) require advance notice.

Payment & Pricing

  • We accept credit/debit cards and cash payments.
  • All fares are transparent with no hidden charges.
  • Deposits may be required for large group bookings.

Cancellation & Refunds

  • Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a fee.
  • Refunds are processed within 5 business days.
  • Weather-related cancellations are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Safety & Conduct

  • All drivers are licensed and trained for safety.
  • Passengers must wear seatbelts at all times.
  • Prohibited items include hazardous materials.

Luggage & Special Needs

  • Each passenger may bring 2 standard-size bags.
  • Wheelchair-accessible vehicles are available on request.
  • Service animals are welcome.

Privacy & Data

  • We respect your personal data and never share it without consent.
  • Payment information is securely processed.

Feedback & Support

  • Customer support is available via phone or email.
  • We respond to complaints within 24 hours.
